COMPANY REFUND POLICY
Version: 3.2 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Department: Customer Operations

---

SECTION 1: REFUND AUTHORIZATION LEVELS

1.1 Standard Refunds (Under $100)
Any customer support representative (Tier 1 or above) may approve refunds up to $100 without additional authorization. The representative must log the refund reason in the CRM system before processing.

1.2 Mid-Range Refunds ($100 - $500)
Refunds between $100 and $500 require approval from a Team Lead or Senior Support Specialist. The approving lead must document:
  - Customer account standing
  - Nature of the complaint
  - Whether a prior refund was issued within the last 90 days

1.3 High-Value Refunds (Over $500)
Refunds exceeding $500 MUST be escalated to a Customer Operations Manager or above. No frontline agent or team lead may approve refunds over $500 without manager sign-off. Exceptions are not permitted. If a manager is unavailable, the case must be held and flagged in the escalation queue with a priority tag.

1.4 Refunds Over $2,000
Any refund request exceeding $2,000 requires dual approval: one Customer Operations Manager plus one Finance Department representative. A formal refund justification document must be submitted via the internal Finance portal within 24 hours of approval.

---

SECTION 2: INELIGIBLE REFUND SCENARIOS

The following cases are NOT eligible for refunds under standard policy:
  - Purchases made more than 90 days ago (without exception review)
  - Digital products that have been fully downloaded and accessed
  - Services that have been fully rendered and accepted
  - Cases where the customer has previously received two or more refunds in a 12-month period

Supervisors may request exception reviews for ineligible cases by submitting an Exception Review Form to the Customer Operations Director.

---

SECTION 3: REFUND PROCESSING TIMELINE

Standard refunds: 3-5 business days to original payment method
Credit card refunds: May take up to 7-10 business days depending on card issuer
Bank transfer refunds: 5-7 business days

---

SECTION 4: DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

All refunds, regardless of amount, require:
  - Ticket ID associated with the refund request
  - Agent employee ID
  - Refund reason code (selected from approved list in CRM)
  - Customer acknowledgment (email confirmation sent automatically)

Refunds over $500 additionally require:
  - Manager employee ID
  - Written justification (minimum 50 words)
  - Screenshot or attachment of supporting evidence

---

SECTION 5: FRAUD AND ABUSE

If a refund request shows signs of fraud or policy abuse, the agent must immediately flag the account with a "Fraud Review" tag and escalate to the Trust & Safety team. Do not process the refund until cleared by Trust & Safety. Processing a refund on a flagged account without clearance is a policy violation subject to disciplinary action.
